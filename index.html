I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Government, with a focus on American Politics and Political Methodology. My research interests lie broadly at the intersection of American politics, political geography, and political behavior.

Prior to Harvard, I earned a B.A. in Political Science from the University of Notre Dame, and an MPhil in Politics and International Studies from the University of Cambridge. I am originally from Platteville, Wisconsin.
